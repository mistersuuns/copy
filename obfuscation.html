Task

Gyorgy Mora, Lead Data Scientist at Ekata-Mastercard needed to edit and improve a summary of his recent research on fraud detection algorithms for their Internal Newsletter.

Process

I read through the original article, outlined key points and questions. Next, beer. I met with Gyorgy at a small Czech bar in Budapest to discuss the broader context of the article's audience, background, and desired outcomes. I then created a new outline, restructured the paper around main points, reorganized paragraph content, and line-edited.

Results

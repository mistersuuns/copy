24px Rocks + Punk Rocks


The ask

Write a narrative about our NFT artworks. Be creative. Briefly discuss our motivation as creators in the NFT space. Have fun with it. Differentiate it from its closest reference NFT products.



Thought process

Adopt a fun, poetic tone to capture the whimsy of the NFT creators. Play on the history and culture of punk rock. Cover the history and current state of NFT creation and sales.


What I did

research NFT space, key players, history understand product's relationship to its closest references


Results

Client was very pleased. 24px Rocks started selling at 0.03 eth and sold out at 0.1 eth. 84 of 100 24px Rocks sold out in the first 30 hours.

Robert quickly researched our topic, translating a broad history into a digestible and entertaining account. By taking time to really understand the project at the beginning, he saved us a lot of time on later iterations. Robert also has a knack for knowing when to shift the tone from serious to humorous and quirky.

Dani Feles - Founder, Designer - Bitraptors
